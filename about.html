About

Art Wrecker is my personal creative playground, a place where I document my journey as an artist through YouTube videos, blog posts, and ongoing art experiments across different mediums.

This project is part journal, part sketchbook, and part public accountability device. My goal is to share my progression, mistakes and experimentation with art. I want to inspire aspiring artists to keep creating regardless of their skill level.

I'm figuring it out as I go. You're welcome to follow along.
